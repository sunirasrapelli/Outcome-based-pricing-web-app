Outcome-Based Pricing Calculator

Sales-ready pricing + performance framing

Price outcomes with confidence.

This calculator helps sales teams pitch outcome-based pricing for AI voice calling: estimate a breakeven outcome price, and show the AI performance needed if a client proposes a price.

How it works

1
Choose your input styleUse percentages if you only know rates. Use absolute counts if you already know volumes.
2
Pick what “outcome” meansLead registration, qualification, KYC, policy issued—whatever the client cares about.
3
Compare AI vs humansSet AI productivity as a % of the human outcome rate and see breakeven + risk.
4
Get a pitch-ready viewBreakeven price + scenario table to show which model wins at different AI performance levels.
